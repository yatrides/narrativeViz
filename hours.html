Average usual weekly hours worked, women 15 years and older

Weekly worked hours by women show negative trends, which is consistent with trends for the overall population. However is important to notice countries that start above regular 40 hours like Chile this is mainly because a Labor policy different than the rest of the countries.

An important pattern is that at same time women participate more, average of weekly hours decreased overall. Lowest countries such as Sweden overall have better work balance and mantain same for women.
